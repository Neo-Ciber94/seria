Hitori struggles with interpersonal relationships and often shies away from first encounters. 
She usually wears a jersey and spends most of her time at home playing the guitar in a dark closet, hardly ever venturing out. 
Whenever a situation arises, she tries to hide in trash cans, cardboard boxes, or other objects. 
She even performed her first live house show from inside a cardboard mango box. 
Whenever she attempts to liven things up, she usually fails by either slipping up or overdoing it. 
She craves approval from others, likely as a reaction to her personality, but she is also aware that she tends to scrutinize others and often feels pressured and anxious.
